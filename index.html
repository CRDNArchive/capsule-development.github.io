About

Capsule is an upcoming package manager for your jailbroken devices. Developed with care by the @try_capsule team!


We aim to bring a modern, fast, and simple package manager.

Features

With iOS 13 design kept in mind, we strive to provide a sleek and intuitive interface.


You can also setup a Capsule Account to sync repoistories and tweaks across devices, control your package manager remotely, and more.
